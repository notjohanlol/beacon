Who are we?

Beacon is an EdTech innovator dedicated to developing accessible, inclusive digital learning tools for students and educators across Australia. Founded in Melbourne, we specialise in building online platforms that break down barriers to education — whether geographic, financial, or physical.

Our team is driven by a shared belief: that quality education should be available to everyone. We are actively growing our technology team and seeking passionate developers, designers, and educators to help us shape the next generation of online learning experiences.

Acknowledgement of Country

Beacon acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the lands on which we live, learn, and work, and pays respect to Elders past, present, and emerging. We recognise the deep and continuing connection Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples hold to Country, culture, and community.

As an EdTech company, Beacon is committed to building inclusive and culturally safe digital learning environments that support and empower Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students, educators, and communities. We actively encourage applications from Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples and are dedicated to growing a workforce that reflects the diversity of Australia.
